What is Masquerade Ball?

Masquerade Ball is an annual formal dance hosted by UC San Diego's Cirlce K. It is the largest fundraiser in the CNH Circle K district and has raised over thousands of dollars each year for our different District Fundraising Initiatives.

Event Info

Date: November 17th, 2019

Time: 8:00pm - 12:00am

Location: Fleet Science Center

Our Cause: Feeding America

Feeding America is a nonprofit charity organization that feeds more than 46 million people through food pantries, soup kitchens, shelters, and other community-based agencies.

Feeding America’s mission is to provide basic food needs to America’s hungry and partnering with other organizations in raising awareness and providing financial support to those in need.

UCSD Circle K will be following Feeding America’s mission by advocating hunger awareness through thoughtful service events, donations, and food drives to better spread awareness of food insecurity within the United States.

Casino Tables

Hosted by Absolute Casino, join us for a night of Blackjack, Poker, and Roulette!

Each voucher is redeemable for 150 casino credits. VIP holders receive two vouchers!

Don't forget to cash out your credit winnings for raffle tickets. 100 casino credits is redeemable for one raffle ticket. If you use up all your credits, you can still keep playing by purchasing another voucher for $5 at the closest raffle table!

Fattykine Flipbook Photobooth

Want to re-live one of the best moments ever? Shanna and Dale from Fattykine will be providing a super cute traditional and flipbook style photobooth to capture your special moments at Masquerade Ball this year.

Regular ticket will get you access to traditional photobooth and VIP ticket holders get exclusive access to flipbook photobooth and a VIP-only line for traditional photobooth.

Food: French Gourmet + Red Sambusas

Celebrate our 21st Masquerade Ball and treat yourself to a delightful slice of exquisite cake from the French Gourmet! Avaliable on a first come first serve basis, try out select flavors of Millenium Chocolate, Tiramisu, and Lemon Buttercream.

Red Sambusas will once again be returning to Masquerade Ball bringing an assortment of their famous authentic curry chicken, potato, spinich and ground beef sambursas!

Mythbusters: The Explosive Exhibition

Stop by the expanded upstair gallery and enjoy an extraordinary experience busting out old and new myths. Based on the popular MythBusters TV show, the Explosive Exhibition features an interactive gallery with original props and gadgets as well as a LIVE demonstration show!

Be sure to complete your Fleets Visit by checking out the Blueprint Room, Running in the Rain, and find out if you can pull off a tablecloth on the table without disturbing the dish.

VIP ticket includes one admission to the Mythbusters Explosive Exhibition. Tickets available for purchase at the Fleets Science Center in front of this exhibit for just $5!

Parking and Transportation

Shuttling

This year, Masquerade Ball Committee’s is introducing San Diego’s finest party bus provided by Cali Party Bus. Get the party started all the way to the ball and back to campus again!

Shuttle: $6 per person round trip, just select the shuttle pass on the ticket purchase form.

Starting at 7:30 PM on the night of November 17th, party buses will shuttle guests from the UCSD Student Services Center on Rupertus Lane. Parking on campus is FREE during the weekends. Buses will take attendees back to campus at 12 AM.
