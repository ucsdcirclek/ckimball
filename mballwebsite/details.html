Alcohol Policy

There is a no alcohol tolerance policy for Circle K members during Masquerade Ball 2019. Regardless of age, Circle K members and their guests will not be permitted access to wristbands for the bar and there will be security checking ID's and wristbands. If wristbands are torn or tampered in any way, those individuals will be turned away and have their wristbands confiscated. Security has full authority to question and/or remove any guests who fail to comply to these regulations.
