Frequently Asked Questions

(Click question to expand for answer)

1. Will there be housing provided for those who are coming from far away?

Yes. By filling out the housing form, you can find a place to stay hosted by one of UC San Diego's Circle K members. Beds are not necessarily provided by the host and sleeping space may extend to but not limited to floor space or couches so bringing sleeping bags or other sleeping material is adviced. You can find the housing form here (Form release at a later time).

2. Will there be transportation to the event venue?

We offer a $6 shuttling service option to the event venue that you may sign up for with your ticket form. Boarding will take place 7:30 PM on the night of November 17th at the Student Service Center in UC San Diego near the intersection of Meyers Drive and Russel Ln. If you are not shuttling to the venue, you may also try and find street parking near the location. Parking information can be found here.

3. What am I allowed to bring to the event?

A list of things that are prohibited from the event includes but not limited to:

  • Weapons, sharp objects, canes, zoot, and wallet chains
  • Water bottles, containers with a screw-on cap(colonge, perfume), beverages
  • Alcohol, flasks, any illegal substances
  • Tobacco products, lighters, matches
  • Markers, glow in the dark items
  • Food items, gum, open mint containers

Girls are permitted to bring one purse but note that everyone will be searched before entering the event's venue. We will also have a coat check in where you can store any lose belongings such as jackets or blazers.

For attire guidelines, please click here.

4. What can I expect from this year's Masquerade Ball?

This year Masquerade Ball will take place at the Fleet Science Museum, a marvelous science attraction with a large fountain out front and many more wonders inside.

During the event, we will have many different features for you to enjoy such as casino tables, a flipbook photobooth, free food, and much more! Features will be added and revealed as the event appraoches so keep an eye out for updates!
