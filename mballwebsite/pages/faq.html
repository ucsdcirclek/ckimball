Frequently Asked Questions

(Click question to expand for answer)

1. Will there be housing provided for those who are coming from far away?

Yes. By filling out the housing form, you can find a place to stay hosted by one of UC San Diego's Circle K members. Beds are not necessarily provided by the host and sleeping space may extend to but not limited to floor space or couches so bringing sleeping bags or other sleeping material is adviced. You can find the housing form here.

2. Will there be transportation to the event venue?

We offer a $5 shuttling service option to the event venue that you may sign up for with your ticket form. Shuttle boarding will take place at UC San Diego near the student service center on Meyers Drive. If you are not shuttling to the venue, you may also try and find street parking near the location. Parking information can be found here.

3. What am I allowed to bring to the event?

A list of things that are prohibited from the event includes but not limited to:

  • Weapons, sharp objects, canes, zoot, and wallet chains
  • Water bottles, containers with a screw-on cap(colonge, perfume), beverages
  • Alcohol, flasks, any illegal substances
  • Tobacco products, lighters, matches
  • Markers, glow in the dark items
  • Food items, gum, open mint containers

Girls are permitted to bring one purse but note that everyone will be searched before entering the event's venue. We will also have a coat check in where you can store any lose belongings such as jackets or blazers.
