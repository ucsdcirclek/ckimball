What is Circle K International?

Circle K International (CKI) is an international collegiate service organization that is a service leadership program of Kiwanis International.

It promotes service, leadership, and fellowship by developing college and university students into a global network of responsible citizens and leader.

It has over 13,000 members worldwide and is one of the largest service orangizations in the world.
