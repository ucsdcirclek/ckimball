What is Masquerade Ball?

Masquerade Ball is an annual formal dance hosted by UC San Diego's Cirlce K. It is the largest fundraiser in the CNH Circle K district and has raised over thousands of dollars each year for our different District Fundraising Initiatives.

Event Info

Date: November 17th, 2019

Time: 8:00pm - 12:00am

Location: Fleet Science Center

Our Cause: Feeding America

Feeding America is a nonprofit charity organization that feeds more than 46 million people through food pantries, soup kitchens, shelters, and other community-based agencies.

Feeding America’s mission is to provide basic food needs to America’s hungry and partnering with other organizations in raising awareness and providing financial support to those in need.

UCSD Circle K will be following Feeding America’s mission by advocating hunger awareness through thoughtful service events, donations, and food drives to better spread awareness of food insecurity within the United States.
